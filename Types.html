History & Types

  • The basic difination of meme is that it is an idea or creativity of creator represented visually in a more humorous way to make a audience laugh or even emotional depending upon that meme.

  • The word "meme" is a shortform of mimema which arrived from Ancient Greek which basically means 'imitated things'.

  • 'meme' is pronounced as meem not mey-mey

  • Memes really got popular entire the world when Youtube was Realesed in 2005 which brought opportunities to creaters.

  • Baby Cha-Cha-Cha, also known as Dancing Baby, was the first meme to go viral on the internet. The meme was created in 1996 to showcase the amazing abilities of the new CGI software by Kinetix Character Studio.
